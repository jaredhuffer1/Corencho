What is Corencho

Cornecho is a unique and premium alcoholic liquor crafted with the finest ingredients in Ecuador. Known for its smooth and complex flavor profile, it's the perfect choice for those looking to elevate their drinking experience.

One of the things that sets Cornecho apart is its origin. Made in Ecuador, it's a product of the country's rich culture and history. The distilling process used to create it is steeped in tradition and passed down through generations of Ecuadorian families.

Another unique aspect of Cornecho is its base ingredient, currincho. Currincho is a type of sugarcane grown exclusively in Ecuador and known for its unique and distinct flavor. This special ingredient gives Cornecho a taste that's truly one-of-a-kind.

Cornecho is best enjoyed neat or on the rocks, allowing its complex flavor profile to be fully appreciated. Its smooth and refined taste makes it perfect for sipping and savoring, making it the ideal choice for special occasions and celebrations.

Overall, Cornecho is a truly unique and premium alcoholic liquor that's sure to impress even the most discerning of drinkers. Its rich history, tradition, and use of Ecuador's unique currincho make it a truly one-of-a-kind experience.
